FAQ — Managed IT Services in Lafayette

Do you provide on-site IT support in Lafayette?⌄
Yes. While most issues are resolved remotely within minutes, our Denver-based team can be on-site at your Lafayette location for hardware issues, infrastructure projects, or anything that requires hands-on work. On-site visits are included in our managed services agreements.
How much does managed IT cost for a Lafayette business?⌄
Our managed IT services are priced per user per month — typically ranging from $100 to $200 per user depending on the services included. Most Lafayette businesses with 5-50 employees find our flat-rate model significantly more predictable and cost-effective than break-fix IT or an in-house hire. We'll give you a specific quote after understanding your environment.
What size businesses do you work with in Lafayette?⌄
We work with small and mid-sized businesses — typically 5 to 150 employees. We're a great fit for companies that have outgrown consumer-grade IT but aren't large enough to justify a full internal IT department. Lafayette businesses in professional services, healthcare, manufacturing, finance, and government contracting are among our most common clients.
How quickly can you respond to IT issues in Lafayette?⌄
Remote support tickets are typically responded to within 15-30 minutes during business hours, and critical issues are addressed 24/7. For on-site emergencies in Lafayette, our average response time is under 2 hours. We prioritize based on business impact — a server down gets a different response than a password reset.
Can you take over from our current IT provider?⌄
Absolutely. We've done this many times. We handle the transition — including documentation gathering, system onboarding, and coordinating with your previous provider — so your team experiences minimal disruption. Most transitions are completed within 30 days.
