Traditional vs. VSaaS

Why VSaaS Beats Traditional Camera Systems

Old NVR/DVR systems are expensive, outdated, and vulnerable. VSaaS gives you secure, cloud-managed surveillance at predictable monthly costs — with the IT expertise to back it up.

❌ Traditional NVR/DVR
✗ Costly on-site servers and recorders
✗ Manual firmware updates — often never done
✗ Footage locked to one location
✗ Vulnerable to physical tampering and theft
✗ No AI — just dumb motion recording
✗ Expensive to scale across sites
✗ Unpredictable hardware failure costs
✅ Workplace IT VSaaS
✓ No servers or recorders to maintain
✓ Automatic software and firmware updates
✓ Remote access from any device, anywhere
✓ Encrypted cloud storage — footage always safe
✓ AI-powered detection and smart alerts
✓ Scales effortlessly across multiple locations
✓ Predictable monthly cost, no surprise bills
