Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is managed cybersecurity?▾
Managed cybersecurity means an expert team continuously monitors, manages, and responds to security threats on your behalf. Rather than relying on one-time tools or hoping nothing goes wrong, you have dedicated professionals actively protecting your environment 24/7.
My business is small — am I really a target?▾
Absolutely. Over 43% of cyberattacks target small businesses, precisely because they're seen as easier targets with weaker defenses. Attackers use automated tools that don't discriminate by company size — if you have data or money, you're a target.
What's the difference between antivirus and EDR?▾
Traditional antivirus matches files against a known list of threats — it's reactive and can't catch new or unknown attacks. EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response) uses behavioral analysis and AI to detect suspicious activity in real time, even from threats that have never been seen before.
How do you handle a ransomware attack?▾
Our SOC activates within 15 minutes of detecting ransomware behavior. We immediately isolate affected systems to stop the spread, assess the scope of encryption, begin recovery from clean backups, collect forensic evidence, and work to identify the attack vector so it can't happen again.
Do you help with compliance requirements?▾
Yes. Our cybersecurity services are built to align with major compliance frameworks including CMMC, ITAR, FISMA, FINRA, HIPAA, and SOC 2. We document controls, maintain audit trails, and provide the reports your auditors need.
How often do you run security awareness training?▾
We run monthly phishing simulations and quarterly training modules at minimum. Training frequency can be adjusted based on your risk profile and compliance requirements. You'll receive monthly reports showing click rates, completion rates, and improvement over time.
