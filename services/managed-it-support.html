Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in managed IT support?▾
Our managed IT plans include 24/7 monitoring, unlimited helpdesk support, patch management, network management, asset tracking, and quarterly strategic reviews. Everything is bundled into a flat monthly rate — no extra charges for tickets or after-hours calls.
How quickly do you respond to support requests?▾
Our average response time is under one hour. Critical issues affecting your entire business are escalated immediately and typically resolved within the same business day. We also proactively detect and fix many issues before you even notice them.
Do you support both Windows and Mac environments?▾
Yes — our team supports mixed Windows and macOS environments, as well as Linux servers. We also support cloud platforms including Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and major cloud infrastructure providers.
How does onboarding work?▾
After signing, we start with a full environment assessment and documentation sprint. Our agents are deployed to all devices, monitoring goes live, and your team gets helpdesk access — all within 5 business days. The process is designed to be low-disruption so your team stays productive.
What's the difference between managed IT and break-fix support?▾
Break-fix support means you call someone when something breaks — you pay per incident and have no guaranteed response time. Managed IT means we're continuously monitoring and maintaining your systems to prevent problems from occurring. It's the difference between reactive and proactive IT.
Is there a minimum contract length?▾
We typically offer 12-month agreements which allow us to fully document your environment and deliver the best results. We're confident in our service — if you'd like to discuss flexible terms, reach out and we'll work with you.
